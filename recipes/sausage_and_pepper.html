Sausage and Pepper

Description

Sausage and Pepper is a classic italian dish that includes high carb pasta, protein packed sausage, vitamin rich peppers and onions, and a bit of parmesan for flavor.

It's the perfect dish to get you going for a long day at work or a quick and intense workout at the gym. I eat it often and love it every time!

Ingredients

  • Kielbasa Sausage
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • White Onion
  • Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and set it on the stovetop to boil. Set the oven to 375 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Cut the onion and bell pepper into slices of medium thickness.
  3. Prepare a baking sheet with a drizzle of olive oil. Place the sausages, onions, and peppers onto the baking sheet, making sure all ingredients are evenly spread out and there are no clumps or piles.
  4. Once the water has begun to boil, pour in your pasta. Let cook for 9-10 minutes or until cooked all the way through.
  5. Place the baking sheet on the middle oven rack for 15-20 minutes, or until the sausage has finished cooking and has reached an internal temperature of 165 degrees Farenheit.
  6. When pasta has finished cooking, strain out all of the water and keep 1 cup of the pasta water set aside.
  7. Set the stovetop to simmer, add in your pasta sauce, add in your cup of reserved pasta water, and mix together with your cooked pasta until there is a light simmer in the sauce.
  8. Once your sausage is finished cooking, remove the baking sheet from the oven. Cut the sausage into pieces of desired size and place in a bowl with the onions and peppers.
  9. Spoon desired amount of pasta and sauce into bowl and mix.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
